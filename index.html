FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What kind of projects do you take on?

From native and hybrid mobile apps to web platforms, backend services, and embedded systems. If it ships software, we can help — from a single feature to an end-to-end product.

How do we get started?

Reach out via the contact form below or email us. We'll set up a discovery call, understand your goals, and come back with a clear scope, timeline, and estimate.

Do you only design, or do you build too?

Both. We run the full cycle — research, UX design, engineering, and ongoing maintenance — so your product stays healthy long after launch.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Yes. We regularly build web services that connect to core and legacy platforms such as SAP, keeping your data flowing across the whole stack.
